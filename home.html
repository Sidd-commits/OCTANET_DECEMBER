Our Mission

KrishiSahayata aims to reduce costs and maximize resource efficiency for farmers by facilitating the renting and sharing of farming equipment. We focus on sustainability and creating a scalable model that benefits rural communities.

How It Works

Step 1: Sign Up

Farmers can create an account and explore available equipment.

Step 2: Rent or Share

Choose equipment to rent or list your own for others to use.

Step 3: Profit

Reduce costs by renting equipment or make money by sharing yours.
